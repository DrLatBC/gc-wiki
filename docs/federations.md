# Federations

Federations are player guilds — a loose social structure connecting players under a shared banner. There is no complex mechanic to federations beyond the attack range bonus during federation wars.

## Federation Wars

When two federations are at war, members gain an expanded punch-down range:

| Situation | Punch Down Floor |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Normal | Your PR ÷ 1.3 |
| Fed War | Your PR ÷ 1.5 |

The 2× punch-up ceiling is unaffected by federation war status.

## Notes

- Federation membership is voluntary and social in nature
- Federations are listed on player empire pages
- Slaves (when the mechanic was active) could bring federation members into conflicts via reinforcements — this mechanic is currently disabled pending rework
