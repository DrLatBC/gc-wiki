# Galactic Conquest Wiki

A comprehensive knowledge base for Galactic Conquest (GC), a web-based text MMO focused on empire building, long-term strategy, and deep combat mechanics.

## About the Game

Galactic Conquest is a slow-paced, spreadsheet-style 4X-adjacent MMO. Mastery is about optimization and foresight, not reflexes. The core loop is empire building — growing your economy, expanding your land, and preparing for or engaging in predictive combat.

**Key characteristics:**
- Turn-based stamina system with fast regeneration (check-in frequently)
- Player-driven market economy
- Deep, asymmetric combat system
- Race selection defines your playstyle fundamentally
- Long-term progression through infrastructure research
